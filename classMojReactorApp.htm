Initialize everything needed to run our app

Called by main() just after logging is initialized (so we can log messages) but before anything else is processed. Always run, regardless of which command line switches we're given.

The sequence of calling for normal operation is as follows:

  • init()
  • configure()
  • open()
  • run()
  • close()
